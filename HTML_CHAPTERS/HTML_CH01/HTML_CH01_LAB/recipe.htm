Cookies

  • Granulated Sugar
  • Packed Brown Sugar
  • Salted Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Table Salt
  • Baking Soda
  • All Purpose Flower
  • Choclocate Chips
  1. Preheat Oven to 375
  2. Mix together the two sugars
  3. mix the butter into the sugar
  4. mix the eggs one at a time
  5. measure the mix in the vanila, salt, and baking soda
  6. add the flour all at once
  7. scoop the butter onto your baking sheet
  8. bake the cookies for 9 -11 minutes
  9. transfer the cookies to a rack to finish cooling completely.
